About Me

Hi, I'm Amo Nkansah, a Petroleum Engineering graduate currently working as an Operations and HSE Trainee at Ensol Energy Gh Ltd, an oilfield chemical solutions company.

With a strong interest in both engineering and safety, I'm building a career focused on operational excellence and real world impact. My background in petroleum engineering, combined with hands-on experience in chemical operations and HSE, haS given me a deep appreciation for the challenges of the oil and gas industry.

I enjoy solving technical problems whether it's modeling reservoir behavior with CMG or building smart tools with Code or the Power Platform .

What drives me is the opportunity to make a tangible impact whether is upholding safety protocols, contributing my quota to field-work or simply making someone's job easier. Every skill I learn and every project I take on is a step toward that goal.

Beyond Engineering and HSE, I'm deeply passionate about History, Innovation, Geopolitical and Socio-Economic issues but above all I am committed to making a significant impact in lives of people. When I'm not working, you'll likely find me immersed in a good book or enjoying a football match.
